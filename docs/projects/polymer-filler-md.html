Polymer-filler MD

GROMACS analysis of polypropylene and brucite interfaces with coated and uncoated filler configurations.

At a glance

System: polypropylene/brucite interfaces
Methods: GROMACS MD, contact counts, interaction energies
Comparison: coated and uncoated filler configurations
Result: coating shifted interaction toward the surface layer

System

The project covers polypropylene and brucite filler-surface models with coated and uncoated configurations.

Methods

GROMACS molecular dynamics, contact analysis, short-range interaction-energy summaries, density checks, shell scripts, and SLURM-oriented workflow management.

Comparison

Coated and uncoated systems were compared with aggregate contact and interaction-energy summaries. The available summaries support a coating-driven shift in the interface interaction pattern.

Figures

Scope

Scope: computational interface analysis connected to materials interpretation and simulation workflow practice.

Reproducibility

The public portfolio includes summary metrics, figures, a final report, and a small reproduction command. Full MD reproduction uses the original simulation environment.

Project folder Notebook

Technical focus

Molecular simulation setup, analysis scripts, aggregate metrics, interface interpretation, and HPC-oriented workflow habits.
