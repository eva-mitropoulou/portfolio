EGFR CADD/QSAR

Public ChEMBL EGFR workflow with RDKit features, scaffold validation, applicability-domain analysis, and one redocking check.

At a glance

Data: 10,593 EGFR molecules
Methods: RDKit, Morgan fingerprints, Random Forest, Vina
Validation: random, scaffold, assay-aware, document-aware
Result: scaffold R² 0.550; redocking RMSD 0.968 Å

Data

ChEMBL EGFR IC50 records were curated into molecule-level pIC50 labels. The model-ready set contains 10,593 molecules for baseline QSAR and CADD triage.

Methods

RDKit descriptors, Morgan fingerprints, Random Forest baselines, Tanimoto applicability-domain analysis, conformal-style uncertainty checks, ADMET-style triage, exploratory custom PyTorch GCN benchmark, and Vina redocking of 5UG9 with ligand 8AM.
